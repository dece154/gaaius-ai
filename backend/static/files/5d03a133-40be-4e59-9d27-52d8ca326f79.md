# Job Title: Dog Feeder
## Job Summary
We are seeking a reliable and animal-loving individual to fill the role of Dog Feeder. As a Dog Feeder, you will be responsible for ensuring the nutritional needs of our canine friends are met by providing them with a healthy and balanced diet. If you have a passion for working with dogs and are committed to delivering exceptional care, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.

## Key Responsibilities
* Feed dogs according to their individual dietary needs and schedules
* Monitor food intake and report any changes in appetite or eating habits to the dog's owner or supervisor
* Maintain a clean and hygienic feeding area, including washing food and water bowls, and disposing of waste
* Provide fresh water at all times and ensure that water bowls are clean and free of debris
* Administer medication or supplements as directed by the dog's owner or veterinarian
* Observe and report any signs of illness, injury, or stress in the dogs

## Requirements
* 1-2 years of experience working with dogs, either professionally or as a volunteer
* Ability to lift up to 25 pounds and stand for long periods
* Reliable transportation to and from the work location
* Excellent communication skills and attention to detail
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* High school diploma or equivalent required; animal-related certification or degree preferred

## Work Environment
* The Dog Feeder will work in a private home or dog daycare setting, depending on the assignment
* The work environment may involve exposure to dogs with varying temperaments and energy levels
* The Dog Feeder must be able to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing situations

## Schedule
* Part-time and full-time positions available
* Flexible scheduling, including mornings, afternoons, and evenings
* Some weekends and holidays may be required

## Compensation
* Competitive hourly rate: $15-$20 per hour
* Opportunities for advancement and professional development
* A fun and rewarding work environment with a team of animal lovers
